## Overview

Buffer Social is a Python-based skill that enables:

- ✅ **Multi-platform posting** to Twitter/X, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, Google Business
- ✅ **Immediate or scheduled publishing** with local calendar control
- ✅ **Media support** including image attachments
- ✅ **CLI interface** for manual posting
- ✅ **Python API** for integration into your projects

## Philosophy

> **Keep scheduling LOCAL** - only push to Buffer when ready to publish.

This approach gives you full control over your content calendar without relying on Buffer's limited queue (10 posts on free tier).
